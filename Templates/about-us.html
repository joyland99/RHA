WHAT DRIVES US

 

We want to break down the "medical language barrier" between the doctor and the patient.
Critical medical information must be presented in a clear and understandable language so
that the patient fully understands his or her choices and decisions.
Ultimately, it is the patient who must make the critical health choices.

We believe that in the future the world of medicine will be dominated
by a new patient who seeks out critical information on the Internet
- and that this will take a new type of doctor who must help the patient
assess the quality of that information.
